## Interpretation

The reformed specification breaks the previous corner result. Some textile US exporters now remain MFN because their incumbent US relationship makes MFN exporting profitable while their QIZ compliance cost is high. Upgrading is no longer a QIZ-only technology; QIZ raises upgrading by expanding market access and adding a compliance complementarity.
